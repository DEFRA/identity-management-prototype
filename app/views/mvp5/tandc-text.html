Registration

The details you provide will be passed to the Defra for verification. If successful, your registration details, including your name and email address, will be stored securely.

Your email address, may be used by the Defra to communicate with you and to forward messages to you in relation to other Defra services if you choose to register for those services.

User ID and password

Your User ID and password will be used to verify your identity and to authenticate information you send.

They will be held securely by Defra and will not be made available to other government departments.

De-registration

De-registration from Defra must be done on the GOV.UK website.

Security

You must always keep your user name and password safe and secret. If you lose both your User ID and password, you will need to contact Defra Online Services Helpdesk.
