Identity test


We’re going to ask you some questions that only you should know the answers to.

This is to make sure someone else isn’t pretending to be you by using your personal details.

We get this information from your credit record. Everyone in the UK aged 18 and over has a credit record if they’ve had a credit account for longer than 6 months.

A credit record typically includes:

  • bank accounts, loans and credit cards
  • utility company accounts
  • whether you’re on the electoral register
  • By taking the identity test, you grant Post Office temporary access to your credit record.

Your credit score won’t be affected.
